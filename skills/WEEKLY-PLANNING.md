---

## Session Structure

### 1. Last Week Review (5 min)
- What did I actually complete? (not what I planned)
- One win worth noting
- One thing that didn't happen — why?

### 2. This Week Setup (10 min)
- Max 3 priorities — nothing more
- For each: what's the ONE next action?
- What's the most important thing if I only do one?

### 3. Clear the Decks (5 min)
- Anything urgent but not important to delegate/defer?
- Any open loops creating mental drag?
